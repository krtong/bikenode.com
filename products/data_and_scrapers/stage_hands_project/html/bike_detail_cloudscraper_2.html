The 3T Exploro Ultra aims to be the most aerodynamic gravel bike on the market, offering a precise combination of stiffness, aerodynamics, and tire clearance, and it’s even available in both 27.5 and 700c wheels. 

To engineer the frame around tire measurements, 3T conducted extensive research, measuring real tire widths (WAM - Width As Measured and RAM - Radius As Measured) to optimize aerodynamic performance. Despite the challenge posed by the large 55-61mm tires and their knobs, 3T focused on minimizing the airflow disturbance caused by the front wheel. They achieved this by strategically positioning the 60mm wide downtube close behind the front wheel, widening as it reaches the bottom bracket. This design captures and redirects the turbulent airflow, enhancing the bike's aerodynamic efficiency. It also creates a unique and progressive aesthetic that many riders will appreciate. 

One notable feature of the 3T Exploro Ultra is its remarkable tire clearance. The original Exploro set the standard with up to 54mm tire clearance; the Ultra version pushes the boundaries further with clearance for up to 61mm tires. This generous clearance allows riders to dive into mixed and rougher terrain without compromising ride quality or aerodynamics.

The bike's size-specific downtube and unique double drop chainstays improve overall structure and rigidity. The threaded bottom bracket ensures reliability and ease of maintenance. Additionally, the integrated fender mounts offer practicality and versatility, allowing riders to add fenders for protection against wet conditions.

The 3T Exploro Ultra is the epitome of an aerodynamic gravel bike, offering exceptional stiffness, innovative design, and ample tire clearance. Its ability to optimize airflow, accommodate large tires, and incorporate practical features makes it the ideal choice for riders seeking a high-performance bike that excels in aerodynamic design while easily conquering mixed terrain.
