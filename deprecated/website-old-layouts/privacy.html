Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025

1. Introduction

BikeNode ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services, including our Discord bot, virtual garage, and community tools.

We respect your privacy and are committed to transparent data practices. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address and username
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Bike preferences and interests (optional)
  • Discord account information when linked

2.2 Virtual Garage Data

  • Bike photos and descriptions you upload
  • Bike specifications and maintenance records
  • Sharing preferences and privacy settings

2.3 Discord Bot Data

  • Discord user ID and server information
  • Message interactions with our bot
  • Role and permission data for bot functionality
  • Server usage statistics (anonymized)

2.4 Usage Information

  • Feature usage and interaction patterns
  • Technical information like IP address and browser type
  • Error logs and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our Discord bot and virtual garage features
  • Authenticate your account and provide personalized experiences
  • Enable community features and cross-platform integration
  • Process your requests and provide customer support

3.2 Service Improvement

We use aggregated, anonymized data to:

  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Monitor service performance and reliability
  • Understand usage patterns and community needs
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

3.3 Communication

We may use your contact information to:

  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send newsletters and feature updates (with your consent)

4. Information Sharing

4.1 What We Don't Share

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 When We May Share Information

  • With your consent: When you explicitly choose to share information with communities or other users
  • Service providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate (hosting, analytics, etc.)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with notification)

4.3 Community Sharing

Information you choose to share in communities (virtual garage profiles, Discord interactions) may be visible to other community members according to your privacy settings.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Limited access controls for our team members
  • Secure hosting infrastructure with reputable providers

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Account Control

  • Access and update your account information
  • Control privacy settings for your virtual garage
  • Manage email communication preferences
  • Delete your account and associated data

6.2 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

6.3 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and operational requirements.

7. Discord-Specific Privacy

7.1 Discord Integration

Our Discord bot operates under Discord's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We only access Discord data necessary for bot functionality.

7.2 Message Data

We process Discord messages only when users directly interact with our bot. We do not store or analyze general chat messages.

7.3 Server Data

Server owners control bot permissions and data access. We respect server-specific privacy settings and configurations.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information promptly.

9. International Users

BikeNode operates globally and may transfer data across borders. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers and comply with applicable privacy laws.

10. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. We may retain some information for legitimate business purposes or legal requirements after account deletion.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes via email or service notifications. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@bikenode.com

Support: Through our support system or Discord community
