Web Extension

Smart shopping assistant for bike enthusiasts

Coming Soon

The BikeNode Web Extension is currently in development and will provide powerful shopping assistance tools for bike enthusiasts across multiple marketplaces and vendor websites.

Planned Features

Price Comparison

Automatically compare prices across multiple retailers for bikes, parts, and accessories to ensure you get the best deals.

Marketplace Integration

Enhanced browsing experience on eBay, Craigslist, Facebook Marketplace, and other platforms with BikeNode data insights.

Specification Lookup

Instant access to detailed bike specifications, reviews, and community insights while browsing listings or product pages.

Community Reviews

See what the BikeNode community says about specific bikes, parts, and dealers right from their product pages.

Saved Searches

Save and track searches across multiple platforms, get notifications when matching items are listed.

Virtual Garage Integration

Easily add purchased items to your Virtual Garage, track compatibility with your existing bikes.

Supported Platforms

When launched, the extension will work with popular bike shopping destinations:

1

Major Retailers

Integration with bike shops, parts dealers, and major e-commerce platforms selling bikes and accessories.

2

Marketplaces

Enhanced browsing on eBay, Craigslist, Facebook Marketplace, and other peer-to-peer selling platforms.

3

Manufacturer Sites

Direct integration with bike manufacturer websites for the latest models and official specifications.

Universal Compatibility

Just like all BikeNode features, the web extension will support all types of bikes - road, mountain, BMX, sport bikes, cruisers, touring, enduro, scooters, e-bikes, e-motorcycles, and more.
