Virtual Garage

Showcase your bikes and connect with your community

What is Virtual Garage?

Virtual Garage is your personal space to catalog, showcase, and share your bikes with the community. Whether you have one bike or an entire collection, create detailed profiles for each with photos, specifications, and maintenance records.

Key Features

Photo Galleries

Upload multiple high-quality photos of your bikes from different angles. Show off modifications, custom paint jobs, and unique features.

Detailed Specifications

Record make, model, year, engine specs, modifications, and any custom work. Keep all your bike information organized in one place.

Maintenance Tracking

Log service history, upcoming maintenance, and track mileage. Never miss an oil change or scheduled service again.

Community Sharing

Share your garage with Discord communities, get feedback on modifications, and discover inspiration from other riders.

Getting Started

1

Create Your Account

Sign up for BikeNode and set up your profile with basic information about yourself and your riding interests.

2

Add Your First Bike

Upload photos and enter specifications for your bike. Include as much or as little detail as you want.

3

Connect with Communities

Link your garage to Discord servers and start sharing with fellow riders who share your interests.

Perfect for All Bike Types

Whether you ride road bikes, mountain bikes, BMX, sport bikes, cruisers, touring bikes, enduro, scooters, e-bikes, or e-motorcycles - Virtual Garage adapts to showcase any type of bike with relevant fields and community connections.
