Discord Bot Integration

Enhance your Discord community with bike-focused features

Overview

BikeNode's Discord bot brings powerful community features directly to your Discord server. Designed for all types of bike communities - from road cycling groups to motorcycle clubs.

Key Features

User Verification

Verify community members and assign appropriate roles based on their bike interests and experience level.

Role Assignments

Automatically assign community-specific roles based on bike types, riding experience, or community participation.

Achievement System

Gamify your community with achievements for participation, milestones, and community contributions.

Community Engagement

Track member activity, encourage participation, and build stronger community connections.

Getting Started

1

Invite the Bot

Add BikeNode bot to your Discord server with the necessary permissions.

2

Configure Settings

Set up your community preferences, roles, and engagement rules.

3

Start Engaging

Your community members can begin using BikeNode features immediately.
