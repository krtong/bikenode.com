Send Feedback

Help us improve BikeNode with your suggestions

We Value Your Input

Your feedback helps us build better features and create the bike community platform you actually want to use. Whether it's a feature request, bug report, or general suggestion, we want to hear from you.

Types of Feedback We Love

Feature Requests

Got an idea for a new Discord bot command, Virtual Garage feature, or community tool? Tell us what would make your bike community better.

User Experience

Is something confusing, hard to find, or not working as expected? Help us make BikeNode more intuitive and user-friendly.

Bug Reports

Found something broken? Detailed bug reports help us fix issues quickly and improve reliability for everyone.

Community Ideas

Ideas for better community integration, new bike types to support, or ways to connect riders more effectively.

What Happens Next?

1

We Review

Our team reviews all feedback within 1-2 business days and categorizes it for prioritization.

2

Community Discussion

Popular suggestions get discussed with the community to gather broader input and refinement.

3

Development

Approved features enter our development roadmap and you'll be notified of progress if you requested follow-up.
