Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add BikeNode bot to my Discord server?

Go to your BikeNode account dashboard, click "Add Bot to Server", select your server, and ensure the bot has proper permissions for roles, channels, and message management.

Can I use BikeNode for any type of bike community?

Yes! BikeNode supports all bike types including road, mountain, BMX, sport bikes, cruisers, touring, enduro, scooters, e-bikes, e-motorcycles, and more.

How do I upload photos to my Virtual Garage?

In your garage dashboard, click "Add Bike" or edit an existing bike profile. Use the photo upload section to add multiple images from your device.

Is BikeNode free to use?

Basic BikeNode features including Discord bot and Virtual Garage are free. Advanced community tools and analytics may require a premium subscription.

How do I connect my account to Discord?

In your account settings, click "Connect Discord" and authorize BikeNode to access your Discord profile. This enables cross-platform features.
